--- title: Frequently Asked Questions ---

Frequently asked questions

  • Will there be a print version?

    • Early access is being offered with people that want to help the book succeed without resorting to a gimmicky crowdfunding campaign. Since we plan to iterate on the book quickly it doesn't make sense to make print versions in media res. We are still trying to figure out an affordable printer that will satisfy our typographic and general quality expectations. Testing printers will be an expensive and time-consuming process and cannot happen until after the book is done. We do have our early access buyers in mind and will offer them discount codes for the print book when it is available if the publisher/printer allows it.

  • Will there be an epub version?

    • During the early access period we're going to be focused exclusively on the book rendered into PDF from our LaTeX. It's something we'll consider when the book is fully edited and ready for final publication, but our experiences getting pandoc to understand our LaTeX haven't been successful so far because we're using custom commands, environments, and libraries. We'd definitely like a non-PDF ebook option that works widely and preserves the attention that has been paid to text layout.

  • Why is the book's price $59?

    • The book will be fairly long and relatively comprehensive in depth. You should expect a book that will take somebody from little to no programming background to having the understanding required to learn how industrial Haskell code works. We're expecting the final release to be 600-700 pages of moderate density. To see the scope of the book, check out our page on completed, upcoming, and planned chapters. We're also taking a lot of care in testing the material. We're not just writing stuff and shipping it after a cursory review from an editor.

  • Why isn't there a chapter on web apps?

    • See our post on this. The long and short of it is that we'd rather equip you to learn anything you want rather than teach you a particular library or framework.
